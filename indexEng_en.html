Welcome to Frisør Hanne!

On our website, you can learn more about our salon and the products we offer as well as the latest news and discounts.

Everything we do centres around our customers, which is why we make it our daily mission to ensure that our customers leave the salon with a smile on their lips and a confidence boost.

We focus on you.

Based on several years of experience, we have obtained great knowledge of which specific haircut, colour, style, and look is the right one for you and your hair. We strive to match your hairstyle with your personality, attitude, and the look you wish to achieve.

Our salon rules revolve around welcoming everyone with a smile and making them feel comfortable at Frisør Hanne. In order to achieve the best possible environment with a high level of comfort and relaxation, we make it a virtue to create a great, cosy, and fun atmosphere.

Please feel free to step by the salon at Boulevarden 11 in the centre of Aalborg at any time, if you seek personal advice or recommendations regarding your hair. We are here to help, and this is the best way for us to evaluate your hair and your specific needs, so that we can guide you to which hairstyle will bring out your most beautiful sides.
