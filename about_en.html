About Us

Are you looking for a hairdresser that listens and understands you, but also provides you with new ideas and great advice?

We are a small, but strong team at Frisør Hanne, which prioritises high quality, good craftmanship, and close relations and dialogue with our customers. We care about our customers and love getting to know them. We aim to provide service and treatment of the highest quality and the best prices.

Frisør Hanne opened its doors in 1982 in the heart of Aalborg. The salon was founded by owner and day-to-day manager Hanne Erichsen. When you walk into Frisør Hanne, you will experience a comfortable and cosy atmosphere, where your wishes and needs are in focus. We will always help you with professional guidance and inspiration regarding your hair. Our goal is to make you happy and satisfied with your hair, so you can leave our salon with a confidence boost, a smile on your lips, and last but not least, fantastic hair.

Frisør Hanne consists of a team of two hairdressers, Mette and Hanne, who both have the 4-year hairdresser education as well as many years of experience within the field. We always work on further developing our skills and keep up with the newest trends in order to guide you and give your hair the best possible treatment.

We are looking forward to welcoming you in our salon!

Hanne opened the salon in her hometown Aalborg after completing the 4-year hairdresser education at the Hairdresser School in Randers and the follow-up practical experience at Frisør Toftild in Aalborg. Hanne has dreamt since her teenage years about being a hairdresser and opening her own salon.

“What fascinates me about the profession is how the most beautiful features in the face can be highlighted by simply making, changing, or coloring a few lines. Every single face and hair is unique, and that is why it is so important to find the exact style and look that serves you the best. The best part about my job is that every day I get the opportunity to make people happier. I love it when I can see that I have contributed to make somebody feel beautiful, and when I can experience how their self-confidence increases along with renewed energy and happiness. Moreover, my job allows me to meet new, interesting, and wonderful people every day.”

Mette started her career at Frisør Hanne, first as an apprentice as part of the 4-year hairdresser education and afterwards as a solid and significant part of the team.

Mette is an experienced and competent hairdresser with more than 25 years of experience within the field. She masters all colouring techniques, and is, in particular, passionate about cutting and styling of men’s hair including the many interesting opportunities and trends within that area.

When you are in the safe hands of Mette, you will experience her calmness and confidence as well as the surplus of mental resources that she has to offer to her customers. If Mette is your hairdresser, then you will be ensured an amazing salon experience, which entails a lot of smiles, a cheerful mood, and a high professional standard.
